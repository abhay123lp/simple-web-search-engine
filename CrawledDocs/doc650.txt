Informatics is a central theme that also enables assimilation and analyses of disparate datasets to support ecosystems research. Research activities are conducted across the major aquatic and geophysical ecosystems in Singapore. Research themes leverage on our core capabilities, building interactively upon each other towards logical translation to applications.
