MENDAKI Scholarship (University) Average Rating: printer friendly rate article MENDAKI Scholarship (University)   Introduction The scholarship was introduced in 1995.  It aims to reward top students who had performed exceptionally well at both GCE ‘A’ level examinations and Polytechnics. Course Preferred courses include Health & Life Sciences, Medicine, Multimedia Design, and Engineering. Eligibility Criteria You are a Muslim who are a Singapore Citizen You are commencing first-year studies in a full-time Degree course in any of the following institutions; National University of Singapore Nanyang Technological  University / NIE Singapore Management University Singapore Institute of Technology Singapore University of Technology and Design You obtained 5 distinctions (including English) in the GCE ‘O’ level examination You obtained 3 distinctions (including General Paper) in the GCE ‘A’ level examination OR a Polytechnic Diploma with a Certificate of Merit Male applicants must have completed their National Service You possess good records of active participation in co-curricular activities (CCA) Value One scholarship worth $4,000 per annum is available each year to first year student The scholarship is disbursed yearly for the duration of the course of study subject to satisfactory performance of the scholar No bond is required but the scholar will be requested to complete 90 days of community work with MENDAKI Application Procedure Application forms may be obtained at the reception counter of Yayasan MENDAKI, 51 Kee Sun Avenue, Off Tay Lian Teck Road Singapore 457056 or click here to download form Completed forms with supporting documents must be submitted to Yayasan MENDAKI by 5 July 2013. Incomplete application will not be processed. For further queries, please call our mainline at 6245 5555.
