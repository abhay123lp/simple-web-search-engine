Getting Around Campus Map NUS Home > About NUS > Publications > SOU Report State of the University Report The State of the University Report presents an overview of the University's progress and accomplishments for each academic year. It covers a broad spectrum of educational, research, entrepreneurial and global initiatives as well as faculty and student achievements and service to community. These highlights illustrate the excellence and dynamism which characterise the University community of students, faculty, staff and alumni.
