There are five tiers of NUS Scholarships awarded to outstanding freshmen pursuing undergraduate programmes in NUS. Interested students should submit one common scholarship application to NUS to be considered for (i) all five tiers of NUS Scholarships, (ii) the NUS Sports Scholarship targeted at prospective NUS students with good academic records and outstanding sports achievements and (iii)the NUS Performing & Visual Arts Scholarship targeted at prospective NUS students with good academic records and outstanding performing and visual arts achievements.
