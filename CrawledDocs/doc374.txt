Careers@NUS 17ff Prospective Students Current Students Staff Alumni Visitors Welcome Prospective Students! We are delighted that you are considering NUS for your further education. NUS has a range of joint, double and concurrent degree programmes with some of the best universities in the world. As a leading research-intensive university, there are many opportunities for our students to interact and work with faculty and graduate students at the cutting edge of knowledge creation in a wide range of fields. We invite you to discover the multi-dimensional educational experience at NUS. Campus Life In NUS, you would be constantly bumping into, interacting with and learning from students and faculty from more than a hundred countries, from a wide range of disciplines from philosophy to physics, from medicine to music. Join us and be a part of a leading global university, which aims to equip and challenge our graduates to make a difference in a globalised world full of opportunities. Welcome to NUS Alumni Family As a graduate of NUS and its predecessor institutions, you are part of the illustrious family of 202,000 alumni. At the NUS Alumni Office, we are committed to serve and enrich the lives of our alumni and engage them in NUS' aspirations. We maintain a synergistic network of 45 Local Alumni Groups and 15 Overseas Alumni Chapters through which we help you stay closely connected with your alma mater and with fellow alumni.       Self-access English Learning Facility Yale-NUS College to add two degree programmes in law and environmental studies The Yale-NUS College announced on 5 January two new degree programmes in Law and Environmental Studies. Graduates at the college will be able to obtain a second bachelors' degree in Law from NUS or masters' degree in Environmental Studies from Yale after another year of study. Senior public officials obtain MPAM degrees at NUS The Master in Public Administration and Management programme, offered by the NUS Lee Kuan Yew School of Public Policy and NUS Business School, celebrated the graduation of its second batch of 77 students at Shangri-La Hotel Singapore on 4 January 2012. NUS Law School partners Tsinghua University to offer new degree programmes The law schools of NUS and Tsinghua University have teamed up to establish new programmes which enable students to earn degrees from both leading universities in Asia. Dr Tahir gives S$30 million to Yong Loo Lin School of Medicine Prominent Indonesian businessman and philanthropist, Dr Tahir, has made a new gift of S$30 million to NUS' Yong Loo Lin School of Medicine. Commemorating 10th year of Master's programme in environmental management The NUS School of Design and Environment held a conference themed "Sustainable Environmental Management in Urban Asia" New facility for catalysis and materials sciences research at NUS A new X-ray Absorption Facility for Catalysis Research is now housed at NUS' Singapore Synchrotron Light Source. NUS-TF forum on community leadership This year's Temasek Foundation Leadership Enrichment and Regional Networking Forum, held at NUS on 2 December, centred on the topic of effective community leadership. NUS welcomes its 9th Chancellor NUS hosted a welcome lunch in honour of the University's 9th Chancellor, His Excellency Dr Tony Tan Keng Yam, President of the Republic of Singapore on 28 November 2011. Kishore Mahbubani named among the world's top 100 global thinkers Prof Kishore Mahbubani, Dean of the Lee Kuan Yew School of Public Policy, was named one of the "Top 100 Global Thinkers in 2011" by Foreign Policy magazine. Nine NUS professors conferred prestigious SNAS Fellowships Eleven distinguished scientists and mathematicians were conferred the Singapore National Academy of Science Fellowships on 24 November 2011, nine of whom were from NUS. Launch of NUS-JTC Industrial Infrastructure Innovation Centre NUS and JTC Corporation have established a new joint research centre, the NUS-JTC Industrial Infrastructure Innovation (NUS-JTC I3) Centre.
