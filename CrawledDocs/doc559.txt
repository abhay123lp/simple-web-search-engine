Featured NUS Scholars - NUS Scholar Programmes & Activities - NUS Scholarships In The News Home > Scholarships > Awards   Awards President's Book Award The President's Book Award is awarded to selected outstanding freshmen Singapore Citizens enrolling into the National University of Singapore (NUS) to pursue a full-time undergraduate course in the following faculties/ schools: - Alice Lee Centre for Nursing Studies - Faculty of Arts & Social Sciences - Faculty of Engineering - Faculty of Science - School of Business - School of Computing - School of Design & Environment The President's Book Award covers: - S$500 one-time cash award - Guaranteed an offer of a 1-semester Student Exchange Programme with one of NUS’ overseas partner universities, provided the student submits an SEP application, and fulfils NUS’ minimum criteria to participate in an SEP No separate application is required for the Award. The President’s Book Award will be withdrawn if the Award Holder accepts an offer of scholarship from NUS, including but not restricted to the NUS Scholarships. The NUS Scholarships comprise the NUS Global Merit Scholarship, NUS Undergraduate (Merit) Scholarship, NUS Undergraduate Scholarship, Kent Ridge Undergraduate (Merit) Scholarship and Kent Ridge Undergraduate Scholarship.Â  Other than scholarships offered by NUS, Award Holders may concurrently hold another scholarship, fellowship, bursary, grant, award or allowance with the President’s Book Award.   NUS Awards for Study Abroad (NASA) There are three categories of awards: NASA (Exchange Awards) NASA Exchange Scholarships are awarded on the basis of academic merit and are open to all NUS undergraduate students who are going on the Student Exchange Programme (SEP). The scholarship amount varies for Singapore Citizens, Permanent Residents and International Students. NASA (Enhancement Awards) NASA Enhancement Scholarships are awarded on the basis of academic merit and are open to all NUS undergraduate students who are going on short term or special overseas programmes, such as summer programmes, research attachment programmes or internship programmes, and field studies. The scholarship amount varies for Singapore Citizens, Permanent Residents and International Students. NASA (Degree Awards) NASA Degree Scholarships are awarded on the basis of academic merit and are open to all NUS undergraduate students who are on overseas Joint, Double or Concurrent Degree programmes. The awards are administered by the faculties/schools and the scholarship amount varies for Singapore Citizens, Permanent Residents and International Students.
