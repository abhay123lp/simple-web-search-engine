Students get the opportunity to intern full-time with companies in Beijing while studying entrepreneurship-related at the highly prestigious School of Economics and Management, Tsinghua University, in Beijing. Beijing is, if not already, the future economic core and centre of the world. The cosmopolitan city, which is the political and cultural capital of the People's Republic of China, has a strong entrepreneurship climate and has attracted people from around the world. In recent years, Beijing has produced companies such as Baidu and Lenovo. Many of the aspiring start-ups are located within the vicinity of the University. Tsinghua Science Park, for example, houses start-ups founded by Tsinghua graduates and is also home to the Chinese headquarters of major technology giants such as Microsoft, Google and Sohu. A wide spectrum of internship opportunities await students on NUS College in Beijing. The current list includes start-ups from venture capital, finance, consultancy and technology sectors.  NCBJ will play a key role in nurturing entrepreneurs who can engage with China. Students will understand the Chinese business eco-system and also gain an in-depth knowledge of the Chinese language, culture and history. If you want to experience the extraordinary entrepreneurial spirit in Beijing, as well as gain first-hand knowledge in the exciting opportunities, China offers to those who dare to venture.
