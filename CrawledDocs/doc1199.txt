Multimedia Publications Awards Rankings Science Sites News Reports Building of Sembcorp's largest energy-from-waste plant begins 30 March 2014 † Photo: Sembcorp Industries Ltd Sembcorp Industries has started building its largest energy-from-waste facility in Singapore. The S$250 million project on Jurong Island is the first to use industrial and commercial waste to produce steam for supply to companies there. The plant will be equipped with two boilers with a combined capacity of 140 tonnes of steam per hour, and is expected to be completed in 2016. This development marks a significant step in reducing the carbon footprint of the petrochemical hub.
