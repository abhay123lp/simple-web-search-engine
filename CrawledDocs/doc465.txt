What to prepare after your application is submitted: Please check your student email account on a regular basis as NOC will notify you regarding your interview schedule. There are two rounds of interviews which are conducted at NUS Overseas Colleges (NOC) office, 21 Heng Mui Keng Terrace, Level 5 (opposite Sheares Hall). The dress code is business attire. Please take a seat at the reception lobby when you arrive for the interview and wait for NOC staff to attend to you. For directions to NOC: View the maps here As soon as you receive an email from NOC, you are required to book your interview time slot online and at the same time, please approach your referees to write at least 2 testimonials for you. They can upload the testimonials in confidence to NOC via http://application.overseas.nus.edu.sg/Testimonial, using your NUSNET Student ID as identification. If you are successfully short-listed for Round 2 interview; you will need to submit 2 testimonials before you are scheduled for the interview. You are also required to bring along a copy of your updated CV and unofficial transcript for your interview. You will be notified of the Round 1 and Round 2 interview results via email approximately 1-2 weeks and 4 weeks from the date you attended Round 1 interview and Round 2 interview respectively.   © Copyright 2001- National University of Singapore.
