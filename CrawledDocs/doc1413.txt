Giving to a School > School of Design and Environment School of Design and Environment The School of Design and Environment (SDE) offers programmes in architecture, project and facilities management, real estate, industrial design, sustainability and environmental management. Since its establishment in 1958, graduates from SDE have been contributing to the design, development and management of the built environment in Singapore and beyond. Your gift to the School will help further its mission to create and impart knowledge, and to foster innovation and enterprise in the built environment.  Each gift will help the School to nurture and groom its students to become architects, building, real estate and industrial design professionals who contribute to a quality living environment for present and future generations.
