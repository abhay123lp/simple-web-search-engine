The Club 21 Scholarship is a merit scholarship supporting the graduate or undergraduate studies of exceptional individuals interested in the fashion, retail, service or creative industries. The Club 21 Scholarship is valued at up to $10,000 SGD per annum for the duration of the course of study, and carries no bond. Applications for the Club 21 Scholarship 2014 are now closed. We thank all applicants for their interest and shortlisted candidates will be contacted by email by June 2014.
