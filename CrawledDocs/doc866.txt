top menu search menu submenu HS Home > Insurance Schemes Introduction Pre-entrance medical examinations are required for all students entering the University. Eligibility All full time matriculated students reading for a first degree, including an honours degree, graduate and non-graduating are obliged to subscribe to the University’s medical insurance scheme. Different schemes are offered for local, international undergraduate, graduate and non-graduating students.Medical benefits are extended throughout the course of study in Singapore only. Benefits cease immediately and students are no longer eligible to subscribe to the scheme when they: 1) pass their final examination and complete their course 2) cease to be full-time students (covert to part-time) 3) are on term Leave of Absence 4) withdraw 5) are terminated 6) reach age 65     footer
