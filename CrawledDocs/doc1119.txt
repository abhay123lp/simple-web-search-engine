Multimedia Publications Awards Rankings Science Sites News Reports New apps to help doctors offer healthcare services beyond clinics 07 April 2014 †† (Photo: Borderless Health Lab) The Borderless Healthcare Group (BHG) has launched three healthcare smartphone applications, Smart Ageing, Heartsmart and Health Abacus, in Singapore through which medical professionals can raise the level of patient education. The apps enable patients with basic medical queries to contact participating doctors for answers without having to go to the clinic.
