NUS-UBC Lee Foundation Student Exchange Award This award was created in 2003 through a generous donation from the Lee Foundation to help fund University of British Columbia (UBC) students to come on exchange to NUS.  The Lee Foundation encourages educational endeavors overseas and recognizes the strong ties between NUS and UBC. Application Period: Closed!
